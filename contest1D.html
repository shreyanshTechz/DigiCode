Task :


You are well aware of the ongoing elections in Uttar Pradesh. Mainly three major parties i.e BJP,SP,BSP Party are the ones which can form the government.You will be given count a of votes in 10 important city of UP. Now to win owner a city these are certain rules:



1. If the number of votes is divisible by 2 , BJP wins from that city.


2. If the number of votes is divisible by 3 , Samajwadi Party wins from that city


3.If there number of votes is divisible by 7, BSP party wins from that city.


4.If the number of votes is not divisible by any of the three, another party wins the city.


5.If the number of votes is divisible by any two number , then both parties form a coalition government in that city..


6.If the number of votes is divisible by all 3 numbers , then all parties form a coalition government in that city..


Then Who will win ? :


Party getting more number of cities under them wins the election.


Note - There will be no case of clash.


Test case 1 :


input will be of array of votes of length 10

INPUT: 10 17 24 12 17 19 35 45 16 20
OUTPUT : BJP
EXPLANATION:

BJP forms full gov in city number 1,9,10 and in coalition in 3,4 city.

SP forms full gov in city number 8 and in coalition in city 3 4 .

BSP forms full gov only in city number 7.

Rest cities lie in other party hands.



Test case 2 :


input will be of array of votes of length 10

INPUT: 5 14 9 6 3 18 33 35 28 27
OUTPUT : SP
EXPLANATION:

BJP forms full gov in city number 2,9 and in coalition in city 4,6.

SP form full gov in city number 3,5,7,10 and in coalition in city 4,6 .

BSP form full gov only in city number 8.

Rest city lies in other party hand.



Test case 3 :


input will be of array of votes of length 10

INPUT: 14,7,49,2,5,7,21,8,10,56
OUTPUT : BSP
EXPLANATION:

BJP form full gov in city number 4,8,9 and in coalition in 1,10 city.

SP form full gov in no city and in coalition in city 7.

BSP form full gov only in city number 2,3,6 and in coalition in city 1,7,10.

Rest city lies in other party hand.
