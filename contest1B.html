Task :


Vishal goes to a stationery, to buy some stuff. Luckily! He gets to know about an attractive offer. Have a look at it. If the purchase is; 1. Upto Rs100, the rightmost digit will round off. (Example: 78 will become 80 ) 2. Between 100 to 1000 and a. below the 50 mark, its rightmost two digits will become zero. (Example: 248 will become 200) b. above the 50 mark, it will come down to the 50 mark. (Example: 263 will become 250) 3. Greater than Rs1000, a 10% discount will be given on the original value and two rightmost digits will become zero after giving the discount. Write a program that will assist Vishal in paying the required amount.

OUTPUT : 80

OUTPUT : 1600
